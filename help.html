Help

1. What kind of footwear do you offer ?

Our collection encompasses a diverse array of footwear styles to cater to various tastes and occasions. For men, we provide everything from stylish loafers and formal dress shoes to athletic sneakers and comfortable flip-flops. Women can choose from trendy sandals, elegant heels, cozy boots, and versatile flats. Whether you're looking for footwear for a casual outing, a formal event, or everyday comfort, we have something to suit every need and preference.

2. What is your return policy ?

We offer a hassle-free return policy to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, you have [number of days] days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. The item(s) must be in unused condition with all original tags and packaging intact. Simply contact our customer service team to request a return authorization, and we'll provide you with further instructions on how to return the item(s) for a refund or exchange. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable, and customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.

3. How can I track my order ?

Once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking number and a link to track your package online. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and estimated delivery date of your order. Additionally, you can also log in to your account on our website to view the status of your order and track its progress in real-time.

4. Can I cancel or modify my order after it has been placed ?

We understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to cancel or modify your order after it has been placed. However, we process orders quickly to ensure prompt delivery, so we recommend contacting our customer service team as soon as possible if you need to make any changes to your order. While we will do our best to accommodate your request, please note that once an order has been shipped, it cannot be canceled or modified. In such cases, you may need to initiate a return or exchange after receiving the order.

5. How do I care for my shoes and slippers ?

Proper care and maintenance are essential to prolong the lifespan and appearance of your footwear. We recommend following these general guidelines:

  • For leather shoes, regularly clean and condition them with appropriate leather care products to prevent drying and cracking.
  • For fabric or canvas sneakers, gently hand wash them with mild detergent and air dry to maintain their freshness.
  • Slippers with removable insoles can be hand washed or machine washed on a gentle cycle, and air dried thoroughly before wearing again.
  • Avoid exposing your shoes and slippers to extreme heat or moisture, as this can cause damage to the materials.
  • When not in use, store your footwear in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and deterioration.

6. Do you offer any warranty on your products ?

We stand behind the quality of our products and strive to ensure that they meet the highest standards of craftsmanship and durability. Many of our items come with a manufacturer's warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. The duration and terms of the warranty may vary depending on the specific product and brand. Please refer to the product description or contact our customer service team for more information on warranties applicable to your purchase. If you encounter any issues with your product, please don't hesitate to reach out to us, and we'll be happy to assist you in resolving the matter.
